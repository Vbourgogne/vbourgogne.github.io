Enlist Today !

Twin-Stick Shooter

 

You play as a former american soldier hired to play in a propaganda movie made to give the people high spirits in the war against the Japanese.

 

Software : Unity

Platform : Computer (Gamepad)

 

The project : Made in a week in a 6-person team without any graphic designer, Enlist Today ! puts the player in the shoes of Butch, a Pacific veteran repatriate to United States after a wound. He’s hired as an actor for propaganda movies. But once he’s on the set, the cardboard scenery and the actors badly made up to look asian make him remember his past battles, and he begins to shoot at everything that moves.

The game also has a “people commitment” mechanic, that increases when the player defeats enemies, and decreases when he gets hit by them. When this commitment reaches a certain threshold, the rate of fire of the player increases drastically, a propaganda slogan appears on his screen and a Sousa military march is played as feedback for the player.

 

What I learned : On this game, I worked on programmation, implementing the shooting and moving mechanics, and tuning them in order to make them in line with the intention designed by the whole team : make the player live an enjoyable experience, allowing him to put his skills forward in a satirical universe. I also worked on the production of 2D assets and Sound Design.

Making this game, because of its short production time and the lack of graphic designers, helped me improve myself in prototyping and in working quickly to create a playable game.
